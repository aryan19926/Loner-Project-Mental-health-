Mental Health Assessment

Question 1: Do you often feel sad or hopeless?

Question 2: Do you have trouble sleeping (either too much or too little)?

Question 3: Are you often irritable or easily angered?

Question 4: Do you frequently have trouble concentrating or making decisions?

Question 5: Do you experience changes in appetite or weight?

Question 6: Have you lost interest in activities you used to enjoy?

Question 7: Do you frequently experience anxiety or panic attacks?

Question 8: Have you been isolating yourself from friends and family?

Question 9: Do you have thoughts of self-harm or suicide?
